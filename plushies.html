Plushie Care Instructions

How to Keep Your Plushies Looking Great

  • Gentle Washing: Most of our plushies are machine washable on a gentle cycle. Use cold water and mild detergent.
  • Air Dry: Let your plushies air dry naturally. Avoid using a dryer as it can damage the fabric and stuffing.
  • Spot Cleaning: For small stains, spot clean with a damp cloth and mild soap.
  • Brushing: For plushies with fur, gently brush with a soft brush to keep them looking fluffy.
  • Storage: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Special Care for Limited Edition Plushies

  • Limited edition plushies may require special care. Check the tag for specific instructions.
  • Consider displaying these in a glass case to protect them from dust and damage.
  • Handle with clean hands to prevent oils and dirt from transferring to the fabric.
